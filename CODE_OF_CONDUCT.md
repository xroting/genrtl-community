# Code of Conduct

This repository is a public community space for technical discussion around
genRTL. Participate with the expectation that conversations stay respectful,
useful, and safe for others.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, direct, and professional.
- Focus on facts, reproducible behavior, and technical tradeoffs.
- Assume good intent, but challenge ideas with evidence.
- Keep discussions on topic and in the right category.
- Protect private information, customer data, and security-sensitive details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Repeated bad-faith arguing or deliberate disruption
- Spam, self-promotion without relevance, or coordinated abuse
- Posting private code, credentials, billing details, or other sensitive data
- Publishing exploit details or vulnerability information in public threads
  before maintainers can assess them safely

## Moderation

Maintainers may edit titles, move discussions, remove content, lock threads, or
restrict participation when needed to keep the community useful and safe.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact a maintainer through a private GitHub channel.
For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of posting
publicly.
